## II. The Beginning (1999–2001)

▪ Many members happened to be in a capella 58 Greene
🡪 FunKtion founder Eric Agustin
🡪 Founder Amit + members Hongshin, Alvin, Steve

▪ FunKtion share some values with 58 Greene:

1. Chemistry is more important than talent. In the auditions process, you take who is best for the group, not necessarily the best voice or the most trained dancer.

---

2. Everyone has a sign on them that says, “I want to feel important.” Everyone should have a specific role they should be accountable for and can take pride in. The group knows and understands each person’s specific role

▪ FunKtion started performing for 58 Greene’s concerts when FunKtion founder Amit Pandya (director at the time) thought that 58 Greene could use a halftime show and that funKtion could use the exposure
